Home Workout Routine For Beginners

Kettlebell

Targetting the Obliques, hold Kettlebells in either hand and sway side to side

Dumbbell

Targetting the Biceps, Keep elbows at your side, and curl 90 degrees from straight out to shoulders

Treadmill

Targetting Edurance, Set to comfortable speed and walk or run in place

AB Roller

Targetting the Upper Abdominals, From kneeling positon put the roller on the floor and stretch as far as possible 5555

Doorway Pull-Up Bar

Targetting the Chest, Pull body up over bar using either overhand or underhand techniques
